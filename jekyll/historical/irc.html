#irssi

Official international channels:

  • #irssi at IRCnet - English only
  • #irssi at Freenode - English only
  • #irssi at EFnet - English only

Non-English channels:

  • #irssi.fi at IRCnet - Finnish
  • #irssi.de at IRCnet - German
  • #irssi.pl at IRCnet - Polish

There's also active non-official channels in other networks, eg. QuakeNet.

If you have something to ask, please JUST ASK instead of asking if you can ask or if someone can help or if I'm there. If you want to be sure that I read your message, prefix it with "cras: " and if I'm not there, I'll read it from my awaylog.

Servers

There are not really many IRCnet servers open to everyone. You can pick a server from the list. Most of them are open for everybody.

  • irc.stealth.net
  • eris.ircnet.org
  • us.ircnet.org
  • irc1.us.ircnet.net

Freenode network has an open policy. Just connect to irc.freenode.net.

EFnet has a server-per-server policy, you will find a suitable server in irc.efnet.net. Most countries have an irc.efnet.TLD.
