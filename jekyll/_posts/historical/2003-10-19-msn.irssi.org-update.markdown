* Changed port 6669 into 6667
* Support the new MSN protocol.
* For the new users:

      /IRCNET ADD BitlBee
      /SERVER ADD -ircnet BitlBee msn.irssi.org
      /CONNECT BitlBee

  Once connected to bitlbee type:

      account add msn user@mail password
      account on 0
